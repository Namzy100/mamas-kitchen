Our Story

Real Indian food,
no compromises

Mama's Kitchen is a family-owned restaurant tucked inside an Indian grocery store on University Ave. That's not a quirk — that's freshness you can taste in every bite.

Everything on the menu is 100% vegetarian, made fresh daily, and priced for a student budget. From Mumbai-style chaats to slow-cooked dals to Indo-Chinese classics — this is the real thing.
